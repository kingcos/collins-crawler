vegetable /ˈvɛdʒtəbəl/ CET4 TEM4 ( vegetables )

  • 1. 

    N-COUNT Vegetablesare plants such as cabbages, potatoes, and onions that you can cook and eat. 蔬菜

    例：

    A good general diet should include plenty of fresh vegetables.

    一份良好的日常饮食应当包括大量的新鲜蔬菜。

  • 2. 

    ADJ Vegetablematter comes from plants. 植物的 [正式]

    例：

    ...compounds of animal, vegetable or mineral origin.

    …来自动物、植物或矿物的化合物。

  • 3. 

    N-COUNTIf someone refers to a brain-damaged person as a vegetable, they mean that the person cannot move, think, or speak. 植物人 [非正式，侮辱] [usu sing]
