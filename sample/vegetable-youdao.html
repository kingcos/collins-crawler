vegetable [ 'vedʒitəbl ]

  • n.
    • edible seeds or roots or stems or leaves or bulbs or tubers or nonsweet fruits of any of numerous herbaceous plant

      同义词： veggie

    • any of various herbaceous plants cultivated for an edible part such as the fruit or the root of the beet or the leaf of spinach or the seeds of bean plants or the flower buds of broccoli or cauliflower
  • adj. of the nature of or characteristic of or derived from plants

    "decaying vegetable matter"

vegetable /ˈvɛdʒtəbəl/ CET4 TEM4 ( vegetables )

  • 1. 

    N-COUNT Vegetables are plants such as cabbages, potatoes, and onions that you can cook and eat. 蔬菜

    例：

    A good general diet should include plenty of fresh vegetables.

    一份良好的日常饮食应当包括大量的新鲜蔬菜。

  • 2. 

    ADJ Vegetable matter comes from plants. 植物的 [正式]

    例：

    ...compounds of animal, vegetable or mineral origin.

    …来自动物、植物或矿物的化合物。

  • 3. 

    N-COUNT If someone refers to a brain-damaged person as a vegetable, they mean that the person cannot move, think, or speak. 植物人 [非正式，侮辱] [usu sing]
